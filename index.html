I am a software developer from Massachussetts currently living in Rhode Island.

After graduating college, I started my career in the insurance industry. While I've truly enjoyed my experience, I feel ready for a new challenge. I believe my professional experience has allowed me to develop numerous soft skills:

  • Effective Communication:
    My experience in the insurance industry involved frequent interaction with clients, colleagues, and managers, honing my ability to communicate clearly and professionally.
  • Adaptability:
    The insurance industry can be dynamic and subject to frequent changes. I pride myself in embracing change and never shying away from a challenge.
  • Problem-Solving:
    On a day to day basis I would encounter many different challenges and had to work on finding different solutions to various problems I would encounter. This required me to thoroughly analyze problems, break them down into manageable components, and devise effective solutions to achieve optimal outcomes.
  • Attention to Detail:
    Accuracy is very important in the insurance industry where accurate documentation is key to good claim handling. I have been able to leverage this strength into writing clean, quality code.
  • Collaboration:
    I would often find myself collabraiting with different teams, including underwriting, fellow claim professionals, and business analysts. My experience has helped me foster the ability to work effectively within a team and contribute to a common goal.
  • Time Management:
    There are many deadlines and expectations as a claim professional. I have been able to hone my time management skills by priotizing tasks, asking for help when I'm stuck, and using resources around me efficiently.
